The trip is 193 miles each way, so \(193 \times 2 = 386\) miles roundtrip. Gas will cost
\begin{equation*} 386 \text{ miles} \cdot \frac{1\text{ gallon}}{22\text{ miles}} \cdot \frac{\$ 3.50}{1\text{ gallon}} \approx \$ 61.41 \end{equation*}
Maintenance will cost
\begin{equation*} 386 \text{ miles} \cdot \frac{\$ 40}{3000\text{ miles}} \approx \$ 5.15. \end{equation*}
Tires will cost
\begin{equation*} 386 \text{ miles} \cdot \frac{\$ 920}{\text{50,000 miles}} \approx \$ 7.10. \end{equation*}
and Repairs will cost
\begin{equation*} 386 \text{ miles} \cdot \frac{\$ 328}{\text{15,000 miles}} = \$ 8.44. \end{equation*}
--> Total cost: $61.41 + $5.15 + $7.10 + $8.44 = $82.10
