Calculating the Sample Standard Deviation.

  1. Find the deviations by subtracting the mean from each data value

  2. Square each deviation

  3. Add the squared deviations

  4. Compute the square root of the sum by dividing by \(n-1\text{:}\)

\begin{equation*} s=\sqrt{\frac{\text{Sum of the squared deviations from the mean}}{n-1}} \end{equation*}
