Solution 2.6.1.7.1.

The radius of the patio semicircle is 3 feet. The thickness of the patio in feet is \(\frac{2}{12}\text{.}\) The volume will be

\begin{equation*} V = \frac{1}{2}\pi \cdot 3^2 \cdot \frac{2}{12} \approx 2.36 \text{ cubic feet.} \end{equation*}

Each bag makes 0.30 cubic feet, so you will need \(\dfrac{2.36}{0.30} = 7.87\) bags. Since we can't buy a partial bag, you will need to buy 8 bags.

8 bags at $6.50 each is $52. After tax, the final cost will be $52+0.094(52) = $56.89
