Worksheet Exercise 12.

Your college GPA is a weighted mean. The grade earned in each class needs to be weighted by the number of credits for the class. Compute the GPA for a student who has earned the following credits:

  1. English 101 (5 credits) with a grade of 3.0,

  2. Math 96 (7 credits) with a grade of 3.2,

  3. Public Speaking (5 Credits) with a grade or 3.6,

  4. Chemistry 100 (5 credits) with a grade of 2.7, and

  5. College Success (3 credits) with a grade of 3.8.

Round the GPA to two decimal places.
