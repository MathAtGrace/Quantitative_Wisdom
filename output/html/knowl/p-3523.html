Toward the end of the Civil War, slaves were freed by Abraham Lincoln’s Emancipation Proclamation on January 1st, 1863, but it was not until June 19th, 1865 that the last group of slaves in Texas learned they had been freed. This is now celebrated as Juneteenth 67 . You can read more about the timeline of slavery in this Jim Crow Museum of Racist Memorabilia 69 .
