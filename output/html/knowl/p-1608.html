Paragraph

The report should begin with a one to three paragraph executive summary. (Busy people like college administrators want to know the “conclusions” up front.) After the executive summary, use appropriate section headings to separate the other key parts of the detailed report. In each section of the report, be sure to spell out key assumptions, explain how calculations are made, etc. Please type out your equations (see help videos for Microsoft Word 22 , Mac Pages 23 , or come ask me if you need help)
