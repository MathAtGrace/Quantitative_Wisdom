Exercise 4.

Gracie wishes to buy a commercial building to expand her business and needs to save for the downpayment. If she can save $4,000 a month, estimate (i.e. do not use a calculator) how long it will take to save $2,000,000 if does not earn interest on her savings. Assume (unrealistically) that the price of the building won't change while she saves.
